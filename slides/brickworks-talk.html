What is Tech for Better?

For our students' final project, they complete a 3-week sprint to build an MVP for a real client, to solve a real problem.

We source projects from non-profits, we work pro-bono, and we create apps that can have a tangible positive social impact.

CAST, Anna Freud Center, East End Trades Guild, Sleuth Cooperative

How can we help non-profits?

What help can we give? What's our aim here?

We can help to kick-start creating a digital solution or service

We can help organisations who are not necessarily digital savvy - they can learn with us, and we'll help them to understand how a digital product or service is developed.

Ideas you want to try out, risk free

We know that some charitable organisations can have a hard time convincing their trustees to try something new or potentially risky. By offering our services pro-bono we hope to encourage more organisations to find ways to be their most effective
