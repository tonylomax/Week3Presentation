We source projects from non-profits, we work pro-bono, and we create apps that can have a tangible positive social impact.

Ideas you want to try out, risk free

Sleuth Cooperative came to us with an idea for a platform they want to build - we helped them to understand their problem space and their users and created an app for them to use as a prototype to gather valuable user feedback. They intend to source funding and iterate on this platform.
