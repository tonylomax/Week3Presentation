Partnering with Founders and Coders

Democratising the digital economy through peer-led learning

Our programme

We run a peer-led training programme in web development, guided by our core values of cooperation, inclusion and social impact.
(see our coursebook)

Supported by employers

The programme is free to students, thanks to the voluntary support of our alumni, and the financial support of our employment partners.

Our applicants

We receive over 100 enquiries every month from all over the UK and beyond. For every one successful applicant, we receive over 25 expressions of interest and 5 completed applications.

Our students

Over 250 students have studied with us since 2014. Three times a year, a new cohort of 16 students joins our 18-week programme, where they work in agile project teams, taking responsibility for Scrum Mastering, QA, UX or DevOps while continuing to hone their coding skills.

Our graduates

As diverse a talent pool as you are likely to find anywhere, with a wide variety of backgrounds and a solid grounding in HTML, CSS, Git and JavaScript.
